My Introduction

I am not your typical programmer. Rather than simply pursuing a career as a Software Engineer, I am driven by a desire to use my programming skills to solve real-world problems. I am constantly excited to learn and explore new technologies. During my internship at Vivo, I was part of a team that developed an Automated Image Testing Platform, which significantly reduced the image evaluation time for the camera testing group by approximately 2 man-hours per week. Additionally, during my time at TikTok as an intern, I contributed to the implementation of a Learning Platform, utilizing my expertise in data analysis. My life goals revolve around making a positive impact on others. With excellent grades in Economics and Applied Mathematics, I have been tutoring for five years since my first year of college. Furthermore, drawing from my 17 years of piano playing experience, I have taught children piano and acted as their accompanying piano teacher.
